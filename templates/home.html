24 / 7 ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE COVERING THE WHOLE SYDNEY METROPOLITAN AREA


Emergency roadside assistance when you need it. No joining fee, No membership, we offer a premium service to get you back on the road with peace of mind. Just pay as you go. If we are unable to get your vehicle going, we will also help with organising your vehicle to be towed to a destination of your choice if needed.

Roadside Battery replacement

All Statewide Roadside Assist rescue vehicles stock a premium range of Batteries to suit all makes and models. We can deliver and install replacement batteries anywhere, anytime in the Sydney metropolitan area.

Roadside Flat Battery service

Our service technicians will safely get your vehicle started with a jump start and can also test the condition of your current battery.

Roadside Flat tyre service

If you have a flat tyre our mobile technicians are able to get you mobile again by changing your flat tyre over with your roadworthy spare tyre. All rescue vans are also equipped with an air compressor if needed. Your spare tyre must be of roadworthy condition.

Roadside Fuel Delivery service

Run out of fuel? No problem, our service vans are able to carry and deliver up to 10 litres of unleaded or diesel fuel to the location of breakdown. This is enough fuel to safely get you to the closest service station.

Roadside Lockout service

If you have locked your keys in your vehicle Statewide Assist mobile technicians will be able to gain access to your vehicle.
